## Changelog

### 2026-07-10

- **Initial profile created** for Matthias Grossglauser (INDY Lab, EPFL).
- **ICML 2025 paper**: "Hierarchical Reinforcement Learning with Targeted Causal Interventions" (Khorasani, Salehkaleybar, Kiyavash, Grossglauser) — proposes a causal discovery algorithm to learn subgoal structure as a causal graph, with targeted interventions improving HRL efficiency; accepted at ICML 2025 (Vancouver, July 2025).
- **NeurIPS 2025 paper**: "Optimal Graph Clustering without Edge Density Signals" (Dreveton, Liu, Grossglauser, Thiran) — characterizes optimal error rates under the Popularity-Adjusted Block Model, showing cluster recovery can succeed even when traditional edge-density signals vanish.
- **UAI 2025 paper**: "Efficiently Escaping Saddle Points for Policy Optimization" (Khorasani et al.) — variance-reduced second-order method converging to approximate second-order stationary points with improved sample complexity Õ(ε⁻³).
- **ALT 2026 paper**: "Ranking Items from Discrete Ratings: The Cost of Unknown User Thresholds" (Villemaud, Sankagiri, Grossglauser) — accepted at ALT 2026 (Toronto, Feb 2026).
- **IEEE Transactions paper (2025)**: "Assessing the Performance of NOMA in a Multi-Cell Context" (Bardou, Gorce, Begin) — framework using Bayesian Optimization to evaluate multi-cell NOMA networks.
