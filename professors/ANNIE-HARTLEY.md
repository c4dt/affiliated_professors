# Annie Hartley

**Lab:** Laboratory for Intelligent Global Health and Humanitarian Response Technologies (LiGHT)  
**EPFL profile:** [people.epfl.ch](https://people.epfl.ch/mary-anne.hartley)  
**Web:** [light-laboratory.org](https://www.light-laboratory.org/)  
**ORCID:** [0000-0002-8826-3870](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8826-3870)  
**OpenAlex:** [a5063796208](https://openalex.org/a5063796208)  

Annie Hartley leads EPFL's LiGHT lab, developing open, auditable AI systems—most notably the Meditron clinical LLM family—for global health equity and humanitarian response in low-resource settings.

### 2026-07-10

- **New (first profile entry):** Established initial profile for Annie Hartley (Mary-Anne Hartley), Adjunct Professor at EPFL IC, leading the LiGHT lab.
- **Key flagship project:** Meditron clinical LLM series has surpassed **100,000 downloads**; the MOOVE evaluation platform has logged **500,000+ clinical evaluations** across **19 countries**.
- **Fully Open Meditron (May 2026, arXiv 2605.16215):** Major new paper introducing the first fully open (data + pipeline + weights) clinical LLM framework; Apertus-70B-MeditronFO achieves state-of-the-art on medical benchmarks (+6.6 pts over base), and Gemma-3-27B-MeditronFO outperforms MedGemma on HealthBench (58% vs 55.9%). Code and models released at [EPFLiGHT/FullyOpenMeditron](https://github.com/EPFLiGHT/FullyOpenMeditron).
- **MoBayes (April 2026, arXiv 2604.20022):** New modular Bayesian framework separating reasoning from language generation in conversational clinical decision support.
- **AfriCAT protocol (April 2026):** Preprint published in JMIR Research Protocols for a computerised adaptive test to advance measurement-based mental healthcare for adolescents in Africa.
- **Evidence & Gap Map scoping review protocol (May 2026):** Deposited on Zenodo covering sex- and gender-specific health evidence syntheses.
- **Federated Clinical Decision Support (Jan 2026):** Preprint on federated modular clinical decision support networks for collaborative learning in resource-limited settings.
- **Fairness-aware voice classification (Feb 2026):** Paper on fairness-aware domain adaptation for Parkinson's and ALS voice biomarkers.
- **TimEHR (IEEE JBHI, June 2025):** Published image-based time series generation framework for EHRs (cited 4 times).
- **MyThisYourThat (npj Digital Medicine, Sept 2024):** Published interpretable federated learning bias detection tool (cited 4 times).
