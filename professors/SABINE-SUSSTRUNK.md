Sabine Süsstrunk leads the IVRL at EPFL, researching computational photography, color image processing, computer vision, and image quality, with recent work spanning low-light denoising, diffusion-based image editing, neural cellular automata, and deepfake detection.

## Changelog

### 2026-07-10

- **CVPR 2026 (3 papers):** IVRL will present three papers at CVPR 2026: *Visual Grounding for Object Questions* (Everaert et al.), *2-Shots in the Dark: Low-Light Denoising with Minimal Data Acquisition* (Lu, Achddou, Süsstrunk), and *Weight Space Representation Learning via Neural Field Adaptation* (Yang, Salzmann, Süsstrunk).
- **New awards (2025):** Süsstrunk received the **2025 UK Royal Photographic Society Award for Imaging Science** and was elected **Foreign Member of the Royal Flemish Academy of Belgium for Science and the Arts (Technical Sciences)** in 2025.
- **Best Poster Award at BMVC 2025** for *Volumetric Temporal Texture Synthesis for Smoke Stylization using Neural Cellular Automata*.
- **Recent PhD completions (2025):** Yufan Ren, Dongqing Wang, and Ehsan Pajouheshgar all defended their theses in 2025.
- **TPAMI 2025 paper:** *Dark Noise Diffusion: Noise Synthesis for Low-Light Image Denoising* published in IEEE TPAMI.
- **Active GitHub repos:** New code releases for CVPR 2026 papers (`wsr.pytorch`, `2-Shots-in-the-Dark`), as well as `Reduced-Spectral-Color-Constancy` (ICIP 2026) for color constancy in hyperspectral imaging.
- **Deepfake detection outreach:** Dr. Quentin Bammey presented the IVRL Deepfake Booth at the AI for Good summit and European Humanitarian Forum (July 2025).
- **Dean of IC School:** Süsstrunk continues to serve as Dean of EPFL's School of Computer and Communication Sciences.
