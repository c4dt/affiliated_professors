Paolo Ienne leads EPFL's Processor Architecture Laboratory (LAP), specializing in computer architecture, FPGAs, reconfigurable computing, dynamic high-level synthesis (DHLS), and electronic design automation.

## Changelog

### 2026-07-10

- **New paper – FCCM 2026:** *NetCilk: Extending Task-Level Parallelism Seamlessly across FPGAs* (Shahawy, Sönmez, Ienne) — extends the HardCilk framework to span multiple FPGAs via latency-insensitive networking, distributing up to 128 PEs across 8 FPGAs with <3% resource overhead and near-perfect scalability on real-world applications.
- **New paper – ASPLOS 2026:** *Graphiti: Formally Verified Out-of-Order Execution in Dataflow Circuits* — introduces formal verification of out-of-order execution for elastic dataflow circuits; artefact published on Zenodo.
- **New papers – FPGA 2026:** *Chext: A Domain-specific Language for Safe and Agile Elastic Dataflow Accelerators* and *Out with LSQs: Custom Circuits for Memory Access Reordering in Dynamic HLS* — both advancing the DHLS/dynamic dataflow compilation ecosystem.
- **Active repo – Dynamatic:** The flagship DHLS compiler based on MLIR has 197 stars and was last pushed July 2026, indicating strong ongoing development.
- **Active repo – HardCilk:** SystemVerilog repo tied to the NetCilk/FCCM 2026 work, pushed July 2026; artefact released on Zenodo.
- **arXiv preprint (Nov 2025):** *Bombyx: OpenCilk Compilation for FPGA Hardware Acceleration* — further extends the Cilk-to-FPGA compilation line.
- **Conference papers (FPL 2025, FCCM 2025, ICCAD 2025, ASAP 2025):** *Compile in Seconds and Run on an FPGA with DynaRapid*, *HBMex*, *DRSA*, *FRESCO*, and *The Rise and Fall of Automatic Instruction-Set Extensions* — broad output across HLS, FPGA placement, and CGRA research.
- **ElasticMiter (FPGA 2025):** *Formally Verified Dataflow Circuit Rewrites* — already garnering 3 citations, signaling community interest in formal methods for dataflow.
