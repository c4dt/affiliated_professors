Touradj Ebrahimi leads EPFL's Multimedia Signal Processing Group (MMSPG), focusing on AI-powered image/video coding, JPEG standardization (including JPEG AI and JPEG DNA), media security, and quality assessment.

## Changelog

### 2026-07-10

- **First profile created** for Prof. Touradj Ebrahimi (MMSPG, EPFL).
- **JPEG AI standard published**: A landmark IEEE Multimedia paper (Oct 2024, 21 citations) describes JPEG AI as the first international standard for end-to-end learning-based image coding.
- **JPEG DNA active research front**: Multiple 2024–2025 papers cover DNA-based image storage with error correction (substitution/indel), fountain codes for point clouds, and learning-based source coding for JPEG DNA.
- **Fine-grained image/HDR quality assessment**: New methodology papers at DCC 2025, QoMEX 2025, and EUVIP 2025 introduce fine-grained subjective quality metrics for high-fidelity and HDR images.
- **Trustworthy AI imaging**: A 2025 SPIE paper explores trustworthy AI-powered image processing for Earth observation and cultural heritage applications.
- **Generative AI for advertising**: New work on cost-effective synthetic image generation for advertisement use cases (SPIE 2025).
- **Pose-invariant face recognition**: Presented at ICIP 2025 (2 citations already).
- **Learning-based event data compression**: Lossless compression for computer vision applications presented at SPIE 2025.
- **JPEG Status Report 2024** published in SMPTE Motion Imaging Journal (Nov 2024).
- Prof. Ebrahimi is Convenor of the JPEG standardization committee; one current PhD student: Enes Eray Demirtas.
