## Changelog

### 2026-07-10

- **New paper (2026):** *Limix: Limiting Lamport Exposure to Distant Failures in Globally-Managed Distributed Systems* published at NINeS 2026 (Dagstuhl OASIcs). Limix is a metadata configuration service that confines strongly-consistent metadata to the same region as the object it describes, insulating global services from remote gray failures and guaranteeing availability bounds proportional to access distance. Companion code released at [github.com/dedis/limix](https://github.com/dedis/limix).
- **New paper (2025):** *TRIP: Coercion-Resistant Registration for E-Voting with Verifiability and Usability in Votegral* accepted at ACM CCS 2025 (doi:10.1145/3731569.3764837), extending the Votegral e-voting platform.
- **Active repos:** `kyber` (699 ★, cryptography library) and `quepaxa` (54 ★, consensus protocol) continue to see updates; new `gd-pure` repo (Grounded Deduction atop Isabelle/Pure, 4 ★) reflects work on the *Reasoning Around Paradox* paper.
- **Recent notable publications:** *ZeroAuction* (FC 2025, zero-deposit sealed-bid auction), *E-Vote Your Conscience* (IEEE S&P 2024, coercion/usability study), *QuePaxa* (SOSP 2023, 7 citations).
